Overview & Strategies — نظرة عامة واستراتيجيات

Reading comprehension is the first part of the BAC English exam. You will read a text and answer questions about it. This section covers the strategies you need to succeed.

فهم النص هو الجزء الأول من امتحان الإنجليزية في الباكالوريا. ستقرأ نصاً وتجيب عن أسئلة تتعلق به.

General Strategy — الاستراتيجية العامة

  • Skim first: Read the text quickly to get the main idea. اقرأ النص بسرعة للحصول على الفكرة العامة
  • Read questions: Read the questions before reading carefully. اقرأ الأسئلة قبل القراءة المتأنية
  • Scan for answers: Look for key words from the questions in the text. ابحث عن الكلمات المفتاحية من الأسئلة في النص
  • Use context clues: If you don't know a word, guess from context. إذا لم تعرف كلمة، خمن من السياق
  • Manage time: Spend about 25 minutes on comprehension. خصص حوالي 25 دقيقة للفهم

True / False + Justify — صحيح / خطأ مع التعليل

In this task, you decide if statements are true or false based on the text, and you must justify (prove) each answer by quoting from the text.

في هذه المهمة، تقرر إذا كانت العبارات صحيحة أم خاطئة بناءً على النص، ويجب عليك تبرير كل إجابة باقتباس من النص.

Strategy — الاستراتيجية

  • Locate the relevant sentence in the text. حدد الجملة المناسبة في النص
  • True: Copy the sentence that proves the statement is correct. انسخ الجملة التي تثبت صحة العبارة
  • False: Copy the sentence that proves the statement is wrong, or write the correct information. انسخ الجملة التي تثبت خطأ العبارة، أو اكتب المعلومات الصحيحة
  • Use quotation marks for direct quotes. استخدم علامات الاقتباس للاقتباس المباشر

Example — مثال

Statement: The internet was invented in the 20th century.

Answer: True

Justification: According to the text, "the internet was first developed in the 1960s and became public in the 1990s."

Answer Open Questions — الإجابة على الأسئلة المفتوحة

You will be asked questions about the text. Answer in complete sentences using information from the text.

سيتم طرح أسئلة حول النص. يجب الإجابة بجمل كاملة باستخدام معلومات من النص.

Strategy — الاستراتيجية

  • Read the question carefully: Identify the question word (What, Why, How, When, Where). حدد أداة الاستفهام
  • Find the answer in the text: Look for key words. ابحث عن الجواب في النص
  • Paraphrase: Use your own words when possible. استخدم كلماتك الخاصة
  • Use full sentences: Never answer with one word. استخدم جماً كاملة

Complete Sentences — إكمال الجمل

You will complete sentences based on the text. Fill in the blanks with information from the passage.

Tips — نصائح

  • Read the incomplete sentence carefully. اقرأ الجملة الناقصة بتمعن
  • Identify what information is missing (who, what, when, where). حدد المعلومة المطلوبة
  • Find the exact location in the text. حدد الموقع في النص
  • Copy accurately — spelling counts! انسخ بدقة — الإملاء مهم

Example — مثال

Incomplete: The main cause of pollution is __________.

Completed: The main cause of pollution is the burning of fossil fuels.

You must identify what a word (pronoun, reference word) refers to in the text.

Strategy — الاستراتيجية

  • Find the reference word in the text. ابحث عن كلمة الإحالة في النص
  • Look at the previous sentence(s) for the noun it replaces. ابحث في الجمل السابقة عن الاسم الذي تحل محله
  • Make sure the number (singular/plural) matches. تأكد من تطابق العدد (مفرد/جمع)

Synonyms, Title & More — المترادفات والعنوان والمزيد

These tasks ask you to find synonyms, suggest a title, or identify the text type.

Finding Synonyms — إيجاد المترادفات

  • A synonym is a word that means the same. المترادف كلمة لها نفس المعنى
  • Read the sentence around the word. اقرأ الجملة حول الكلمة
  • Look for words with similar meaning. ابحث عن كلمات بنفس المعنى

Choosing a Title — اختيار عنوان

  • The title should reflect the main idea. يجب أن يعكس العنوان الفكرة الرئيسية
  • It should be short and clear. يجب أن يكون قصيراً وواضحاً
  • Re-read the first and last paragraphs. أعد قراءة الفقرة الأولى والأخيرة

All 8 Tenses — جميع الأزمنة الثمانية

English has 8 main tenses. Each has a form, use, and signal words. Master them for a high score. الإنجليزية لها 8 أزمنة رئيسية. لكل زمن صيغة واستعمال وكلمات دالة.

1. Simple Present — المضارع البسيط

Form & Use

Form: Subject + Verb (s/es for he/she/it)

Use: Habits, facts, routines (عادة، حقيقة، روتين)

Signal words: always, usually, often, sometimes, never, every day, generally

Example: She wakes up at 7 am every day. — هي تستيقظ في الساعة 7 كل يوم

Negative: Subject + do/does + not + verb

Question: Do/Does + subject + verb?

I/You/We/They + work
He/She/It + works
I do not (don't) work
Does he work?

2. Present Continuous — المضارع المستمر

Form & Use

Form: Subject + am/is/are + verb-ing

Use: Actions happening NOW or temporary situations (فعل يحدث الآن أو وضع مؤقت)

Signal words: now, right now, at the moment, today, this week, look!, listen!

Example: They are studying for the exam now. — هم يدرسون الآن للامتحان

I + am working
He/She/It + is working
We/You/They + are working

3. Simple Past — الماضي البسيط

Form & Use

Form: Subject + V2 (past form — regular +ed / irregular form)

Use: Completed actions in the past (فعل منته في الماضي)

Signal words: yesterday, last (week/year/month), ago, in 2010, then

Example: He visited Paris last summer. — هو زار باريس الصيف الماضي

Negative: Subject + did + not + verb (base)

Question: Did + subject + verb (base)?

I/You/He/She/It/We/They + worked / went
I did not (didn't) work
Did you work?

4. Past Continuous — الماضي المستمر

Form & Use

Form: Subject + was/were + verb-ing

Use: Actions in progress at a specific past time, interrupted actions (فعل كان مستمراً في الماضي)

Signal words: while, when, at that moment, at 5pm yesterday

Example: I was reading when he called. — كنت أقرأ عندما اتصل

I/He/She/It + was reading
We/You/They + were reading

5. Present Perfect — المضارع التام

Form & Use

Form: Subject + have/has + V3 (past participle)

Use: Past actions with present relevance, experiences, unfinished time periods

Signal words: ever, never, just, already, yet, since, for, recently, so far

Example: She has visited London twice. — هي زارت لندن مرتين

I/You/We/They + have seen
He/She/It + has seen
Have you ever seen...?

⚠ SINCE = point in time (since 2010)
⚠ FOR = duration (for 5 years)

6. Present Perfect Continuous — المضارع التام المستمر

Form & Use

Form: Subject + have/has + been + verb-ing

Use: Actions that started in the past and continue to now, emphasis on duration

Signal words: for, since, all day, lately, recently

Example: It has been raining all day. — كانت تمطر طوال اليوم

7. Simple Future — المستقبل البسيط

Forms & Use

Form 1 (will): Subject + will + verb (base) — spontaneous decisions, promises, predictions

Form 2 (going to): Subject + am/is/are + going to + verb — planned intentions

Signal words: tomorrow, next week, soon, in the future, later

Example: I will help you. / She is going to study medicine.

8. Past Perfect — الماضي التام

Form & Use

Form: Subject + had + V3 (past participle)

Use: Action completed BEFORE another past action (فعل تم قبل فعل آخر في الماضي)

Signal words: after, before, already, never, by the time, when

Example: When I arrived, they had already left. — عندما وصلت، كانوا قد غادروا بالفعل

Passive Voice — المبني للمجهول

Passive voice focuses on the action, not who does it. Used in formal and academic English. المبني للمجهول يركز على الفعل وليس على الفاعل.

Formation — الصياغة

Active: Subject + Verb + Object

Passive: Object + be (conjugated) + V3 (past participle) + (by agent)

TenseActivePassive
Simple PresentHe writes a letterA letter is written (by him)
Present ContinuousHe is writing a letterA letter is being written
Simple PastHe wrote a letterA letter was written (by him)
Past ContinuousHe was writing a letterA letter was being written
Present PerfectHe has written a letterA letter has been written
Past PerfectHe had written a letterA letter had been written
Simple FutureHe will write a letterA letter will be written
ModalHe can write a letterA letter can be written

When to Use Passive — متى نستخدم المبني للمجهول

  • When the agent is unknown (عندما يكون الفاعل غير معروف): My bike was stolen.
  • When the agent is obvious (عندما يكون الفاعل معلوماً): He was arrested. (by police)
  • In scientific/academic writing: The experiment was conducted.
  • To emphasize the action: A new hospital will be built.
BAC Tip: Passive is common in the grammar gap-fill and rewriting exercises.

Reporting Questions — نقل الأسئلة

  • Yes/No questions: He asked if / whether + sentence
    "Do you like it?" → He asked if I liked it.
  • Wh- questions: He asked + question word + sentence
    "Where do you live?" → He asked where I lived.

Reporting Commands — نقل الأوامر

He told/asked me to + verb (base)

"Open the door" → He told me to open the door.

"Don't be late" → He told me not to be late.

Conditionals & Wish — الجمل الشرطية و "أتمنى"

Conditional sentences express conditions and results. There are 4 main types. الجمل الشرطية تعبر عن الشرط والنتيجة.

Type 0 — General Truth (حقيقة عامة)

Form: If + present simple, present simple

Use: Scientific facts, general truths (حقائق علمية)

If you heat water to 100°C, it boils. — إذا سخنت الماء إلى 100 درجة، يغلي

Type 1 — Real/Future Possible (ممكن حدوثه في المستقبل)

Form: If + present simple, will + base verb

Use: Likely future situations (مواقف محتملة في المستقبل)

If I study hard, I will pass the exam. — إذا درست بجد، سأنجح في الامتحان

Type 2 — Unreal Present (غير حقيقي في الحاضر)

Form: If + past simple, would + base verb

Use: Imaginary situations, unlikely in the present (مواقف خيالية حالياً)

If I were you, I would accept the offer. — لو كنت مكانك، لقبلت العرض

⚠ Important: Use were for all subjects in Type 2 (even I/he/she/it).

Type 3 — Unreal Past (غير حقيقي في الماضي / ندم)

Form: If + past perfect, would have + V3

Use: Regret about the past, impossible to change (أسف على الماضي)

If I had studied harder, I would have passed the exam. — لو درست بجد أكثر، لكنت نجحت في الامتحان

I Wish — أتمنى / ليت

Wish aboutFormExample
Present (want to change)wish + past simpleI wish I knew the answer. (ليتني أعرف الجواب)
Past (regret)wish + past perfectI wish I had studied. (ليتني درست)
Future (want change)wish + would + verbI wish you would listen. (ليتك تستمع)
BAC Tip: Conditionals are a favorite in the BAC exam. Know the difference between Type 1, 2, and 3!

Modal Verbs — الأفعال الناقصة

Modals are auxiliary verbs that express possibility, necessity, permission, ability, etc. الأفعال الناقصة تعبر عن الإمكانية، الضرورة، الإذن، القدرة، إلخ.

Key Rules — قواعد مهمة

  • Modals are followed by base verb (without to): You must go.
  • No "s" in third person: He can go (not goes).
  • Negative: add not: cannot (can't), must not (mustn't)
  • Mustn't = prohibition (ممنوع) vs don't have to = not necessary (ليس ضرورياً)

Defining vs Non-Defining — المحدد وغير المحدد

  • Defining (essential): No commas. Needed to identify. بدون فاصلة، ضروري لتحديد المعنى
    The woman who lives next door is nice. (Which woman? The one next door)
  • Non-defining (extra info): With commas. معلومات إضافية
    My mother, who is 50, is a teacher. (Extra info about my mother)
  • In non-defining, that cannot replace who/which. في غير المحدد، "that" لا يمكن أن تحل محل who/which
BAC Tip: "Who" is for people, "which" for things. "That" can replace both in defining clauses.

Gerund & Infinitive — المصدر واسم الفعل

Some verbs are followed by gerund (-ing), others by infinitive (to + verb). بعض الأفعال تتبع باسم الفعل، وأخرى بالمصدر.

Verbs + Gerund (verb-ing) — الأفعال التي تتبع باسم الفعل

  • enjoy, avoid, suggest, consider, mind, finish, practice, imagine, risk, admit, deny, postpone, keep
  • I enjoy reading books. / He avoided meeting her.

  • want, hope, decide, expect, plan, promise, offer, refuse, agree, manage, fail, pretend

Writing Overview — نظرة عامة على التعبير

The writing section is worth 10 points in the BAC exam. You must write a well-organized paragraph on a given topic. جزء التعبير يستحق 10 نقاط.

Writing Structure — بنية الكتابة

  • Introduction: Topic sentence — state your main idea. جملة افتتاحية: اذكر فكرتك الرئيسية
  • Body: 2-3 supporting sentences with examples/explanations. 2-3 جمل داعمة مع أمثلة
  • Conclusion: Restate your main idea or give a final thought. أعد صياغة الفكرة الرئيسية

General Tips — نصائح عامة

  • Plan your paragraph for 2 minutes before writing. خطط لفقرتك لمدة دقيقتين قبل الكتابة
  • Use linking words (first, moreover, however, in conclusion). استخدم كلمات الربط
  • Write 100-150 words for BAC. اكتب 100-150 كلمة
  • Check grammar, spelling, and punctuation. تأكد من القواعد والإملاء والعلامات
  • Use the Bridge templates for each writing type. استخدم قوالب Bridge لكل نوع

Causes & Solutions — الأسباب والحلول

Explain causes of a problem and suggest solutions. شرح أسباب مشكلة واقتراح حلول.

BRIDGE Template

Introduction: __________ is a serious problem that affects many people. There are several causes of this problem and some possible solutions.

Body (Causes): The main causes of __________ are __________. First, __________. Second, __________.

Body (Solutions): To solve this problem, some measures should be taken. First, __________. Second, __________.

Conclusion: In conclusion, __________ is a serious problem, but with collective efforts, we can overcome it.

Topic: Pollution

Causes: industrial waste, car emissions, plastic use

Solutions: use renewable energy, plant trees, recycle

Advantages & Disadvantages — المزايا والعيوب

Discuss both positive and negative aspects of a topic. مناقشة الجوانب الإيجابية والسلبية لموضوع.

BRIDGE Template

Introduction: __________ has both advantages and disadvantages. In this paragraph, I will discuss some of them.

Body (Advantages): On the one hand, __________ has many advantages. First, __________. Second, __________.

Body (Disadvantages): On the other hand, there are some disadvantages. First, __________. Second, __________.

Conclusion: In conclusion, despite its disadvantages, I believe the advantages of __________ outweigh the disadvantages.

Topic: Social Media

Advantages: connects people, spreads information, business opportunities

Disadvantages: addiction, privacy issues, fake news

Informal Email/Letter — البريد الإلكتروني/الرسالة غير الرسمية

Write to a friend or family member. Informal tone. تكتب لصديق أو قريب بنبرة غير رسمية.

BRIDGE Template

Dear __________,

How are you? I hope you are doing well. I'm writing to __________.

Anyway, that's all for now. I'm looking forward to hearing from you.

Take care,

__________

Key Features — الخصائص

  • Use contractions (I'm, don't, isn't). استخدم الاختصارات
  • Friendly greeting and closing. تحية وختام ودية
  • Personal tone. نبرة شخصية

Formal Email/Letter — البريد الإلكتروني/الرسالة الرسمية

Write to an official person or organization. Formal tone. تكتب لشخص رسمي أو مؤسسة بنبرة رسمية.

BRIDGE Template

Dear Sir/Madam,

I am writing to express my __________ / to complain about __________ / to apply for __________.

First of all, __________. Furthermore, __________.

I would appreciate it if you could __________.

I look forward to hearing from you.

Yours faithfully,

__________

Key Features — الخصائص

  • No contractions. لا تستخدم الاختصارات
  • Formal greeting (Dear Sir/Madam, To Whom It May Concern). تحية رسمية
  • Polite expressions (I would appreciate, I would be grateful). تعابير مهذبة
  • "Yours faithfully" if you start with "Dear Sir/Madam"; "Yours sincerely" if you use the person's name

Describe a Person — وصف شخص

Describe someone's physical appearance, personality, and your relationship. وصف المظهر الخارجي والشخصية والعلاقة.

BRIDGE Template

Introduction: The person I admire most is __________. He/She is my __________.

Body (Appearance): Physically, he/she is __________. He/She has __________.

Body (Personality): In terms of personality, he/she is __________. For example, __________.

Body (Why): I admire him/her because __________.

Conclusion: To conclude, __________ is a wonderful person who has taught me a lot.

Describe a Place — وصف مكان

Describe a place — its location, features, atmosphere, and why it's special. وصف مكان: موقعه، مميزاته، أجواؤه.

BRIDGE Template

Introduction: The place I would like to describe is __________, located in __________.

Body (Location/Appearance): It is located __________. It is __________ (size/color/style).

Body (Atmosphere): The atmosphere there is __________. You can __________.

Body (Why special): What makes this place special is __________.

Conclusion: I really love this place and I recommend everyone to visit it.

BAC Exam Strategy — استراتيجية امتحان الباكالوريا

A step-by-step strategy to maximize your score in the BAC English exam. استراتيجية خطوة بخطوة لتحقيق أقصى درجة.

Exam Structure — بنية الامتحان

PartContentTime
Part 1: ComprehensionRead text + answer questions (T/F, open Q, reference, title)25 min
Part 2: GrammarGap-fill exercises (tenses, passive, modals, etc.)20 min
Part 3: VocabularyComplete with given words, functions, phrasal verbs10 min
Part 4: WritingWrite a paragraph (100-150 words)25 min

Time Management — إدارة الوقت

  • 15 min: Read the text + answer comprehension questions. اقرأ النص + أجب عن أسئلة الفهم
  • 10 min: Grammar & vocabulary exercises. تمارين القواعد والمفردات
  • 25 min: Plan and write your paragraph. خطط واكتب فقرتك
  • 5 min: Review your answers. راجع إجاباتك

Key Tips — نصائح أساسية

  • Read the comprehension questions FIRST before reading the text. اقرأ الأسئلة أولاً قبل النص
  • Use your own words for open questions (but you can quote for T/F). استخدم كلماتك الخاصة للأسئلة المفتوحة
  • In writing, use the BRIDGE templates. في التعبير، استخدم قوالب BRIDGE
  • Always justify T/F answers with a quote from the text. دائماً علل إجابات الصحيح/خطأ باقتباس
  • Check spelling and grammar at the end. تأكد من الإملاء والقواعد في النهاية
  • Don't leave any question blank — guess if you must. لا تترك أي سؤال فارغاً
  • Write legibly. اكتب بخط واضح

Scoring — التوزيع

SectionPoints
Comprehension (10)2 T/F + 2 open Q + 1 reference + 1 title + 2 synonyms + 2 complete
Grammar (5)5 gap-fill items
Vocabulary (5)5 items (functions, phrasal, collocations)
Writing (10)Content + organization + language + accuracy

20 Common Mistakes — 20 خطأ شائعاً

Avoid these frequent errors to improve your score. تجنب هذه الأخطاء الشائعة لتحسين درجتك.

  1. Subject-verb agreement: He go → He goes. (الفعل مع الفاعل)
  2. Missing -s in 3rd person: She work → She works.
  3. Wrong past form: I goed → I went.
  4. Confusing his/her: Ahmed loves her mother → Ahmed loves his mother.
  5. Double negative: I don't have nothing → I don't have anything / I have nothing.
  6. Wrong preposition: Married with → Married to. / Good on → Good at.
  7. Missing articles: She is teacher → She is a teacher.
  8. Confusing its/it's: Its raining → It's raining. (it's = it is)
  9. Confusing there/their/they're: Their is a problem → There is a problem.
  10. Wrong tense sequence: If I will see him → If I see him, I will tell.
  11. Forgetting auxiliary in questions: You like it? → Do you like it?
  12. Word order in reported questions: He asked where do I live → He asked where I lived.
  13. Confusing since/for: since 5 years → for 5 years / since 2019.
  14. Using "more" + -er: more better → better.
  15. Missing "to" after want: I want go → I want to go.
  16. Wrong plural: Childs → Children. / Mans → Men.
  17. Confusing advice/advise: He gave me good advise → He gave me good advice.
  18. Wrong use of "make" vs "do": Make homework → Do homework. / Do a mistake → Make a mistake.
  19. Wrong conditional: If I was you → If I were you.
  20. Spelling -ing forms: runing → running. / writeing → writing.

Answer Keys & Corrections — مفاتيح الإجابات والتصحيحات

Review corrected national exam answers to understand common patterns and scoring. راجع إجابات الامتحانات الوطنية المصححة لفهم الأنماط الشائعة والتنقيط.

Comprehension Correction — تصحيح الفهم

  • T/F: Must quote from the text word-for-word. يجب الاقتباس حرفياً من النص
  • Open Q: Answer in complete sentence. الإجابة بجملة كاملة
  • Reference: Identify the exact noun or idea. تحديد الاسم أو الفكرة بالضبط

Grammar Correction — تصحيح القواعد

  • Spelling of irregular verbs counts. إملاء الأفعال الشاذة مهم
  • Tense must match the time expression. الزمن يجب أن يتناسب مع ظرف الزمان

Writing Correction — تصحيح التعبير

  • Content (4 pts): Relevance to topic. ارتباط بالموضوع
  • Organization (2 pts): Structure, linking words. التنظيم، كلمات الربط
  • Language (2 pts): Grammar, vocabulary. القواعد، المفردات
  • Accuracy (2 pts): Spelling, punctuation. الإملاء، علامات الترقيم

Top Proverbs for BAC — أهم الأمثال للباكالوريا

  1. "Where there is a will, there is a way." — من أراد شيئاً وجد له طريقة. إذا بغيت شي لقيت طريق
  2. "Practice makes perfect." — التمرين يصنع الإتقان. التمرين كامل
  3. "Actions speak louder than words." — الأفعال أبلغ من الأقوال. الأفعال أهم من الكلام
  4. "Better late than never." — أن تأتي متأخراً أفضل من ألا تأتي أبداً. المهم وصلتي حتى إذا تأخرتي
  5. "Prevention is better than cure." — الوقاية خير من العلاج. الوقاية خير من الدواء
  6. "No pain, no gain." — لا ألم لا ربح. بلا تعب ما كاين ربح
  7. "United we stand, divided we fall." — متحدون نقوى، متفرقون نسقط. بالوحدة نقوى وبالفرقة نضعف
  8. "The early bird catches the worm." — الطائر المبكر يصطاد الدودة. اللي باكر كيجيب الدودة
  9. "All that glitters is not gold." — كل ما يلمع ليس ذهباً. اللي كيلمع ما كلو ذهب
  10. "A friend in need is a friend indeed." — الصديق وقت الضيق. الصاحب فاش كتحتاج ليه هو الصاحب الحقيقي
